Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Soma Studio respects your right to control your personal data. If you would like us to delete the data we have collected about you — including information obtained through your connected Facebook Page or Instagram Business account — you may do so using either of the methods below.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

Log in to Soma Studio and navigate to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete My Account. Confirming this action will:

  • Revoke Soma Studio's access to your connected Facebook Page and Instagram accounts
  • Permanently delete your profile information, saved content, and publishing history from our systems
  • Remove any access tokens associated with your account

This process is typically completed immediately and no later than 30 days from your request.

Option 2: Email Request

If you are unable to access the app, you may request deletion by emailing priyadarshini.sajjanshetty@firstqa.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request," including:

  • The email address or username associated with your Soma Studio account
  • The Facebook Page or Instagram account you connected (if applicable)

We will verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days, and will confirm by email once done.

What Gets Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, we permanently remove:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Access tokens and permissions linked to your Facebook or Instagram accounts
  • Content, drafts, and publishing history stored within Soma Studio
  • Any analytics or usage data tied to your account

Data deletion within Soma Studio does not delete content already published to Facebook or Instagram; that content must be removed directly through those platforms.

Revoking Access via Facebook/Instagram Directly

You can also remove Soma Studio's access at any time from your Facebook or Instagram settings, under Settings → Apps and Websites (Facebook) or Settings → Apps and Websites → Active (Instagram), without needing to contact us. We recommend also submitting the deletion request above so your data is removed from our servers as well.

Contact

For any questions regarding data deletion, contact priyadarshini.sajjanshetty@firstqa.com.
